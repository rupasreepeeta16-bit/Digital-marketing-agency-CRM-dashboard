### **CRM Dashboard for a Digital Marketing Agency**



**Project Overview**



The CRM Dashboard for a Digital Marketing Agency was designed to centralize lead management, client relationships, task tracking, and business analytics within a unified platform. The design is built upon core UI/UX principles such as clear information architecture, intuitive navigation, visual consistency, scalability, and data-driven decision-making, ensuring an efficient and user-centric experience.



**Workflow Logic**



The dashboard is structured around the agency's operational workflow, enabling teams to manage leads, clients, follow-ups, and performance metrics through a unified system. This workflow-driven approach enhances operational visibility, improves collaboration, and supports more informed business decisions.



**Project Scope**



Dashboard Overview

&#x20;   Provides a comprehensive overview of agency performance through key performance indicators, lead activity, revenue trends, and priority tasks, enabling quick assessment of daily operations.



&#x20;Lead Management

&#x20;   Facilitates the organization and tracking of leads across different stages of the sales pipeline, improving lead visibility, engagement monitoring, and conversion management.



&#x20;Active Clients

&#x20;   Centralizes client information, engagement history, and relationship data, allowing teams to efficiently manage ongoing client interactions and project activities.



&#x20;Task \& Follow-Ups

&#x20;   Supports task scheduling, follow-up management, and activity tracking to ensure timely execution of responsibilities and improved operational accountability.



&#x20;Reports \& Analytics

&#x20;   Delivers actionable insights through revenue analysis, lead source distribution, and performance metrics, enabling data-driven evaluation and strategic decision-making.



&#x20;Design Approach

&#x20;  The interface was designed using modern SaaS design methodologies with a strong emphasis on usability, readability, and scalability. Consistent layouts, structured content organization, and intuitive data visualization contribute to a seamless and efficient user experience.



&#x20;**Tools Used**



&#x20;   Figma



&#x20;**Result**



The final CRM Dashboard provides a centralized platform for managing leads, clients, tasks, and analytics. The design improves operational visibility and supports efficient workflow management for digital marketing agencies.
